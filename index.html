Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our security and electrical services.

What areas do you service?

We provide security and electrical services throughout South Africa. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific area.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we offer free consultations and quotes for all our services. Simply contact us via phone, email, or our contact form to schedule an appointment.

Are your installations certified?

Yes, all our electrical installations come with Certificates of Compliance (COC). Our team consists of fully qualified and certified professionals.

How long does installation take?

Installation times vary depending on the service. Simple installations like gate motors can be completed in a few hours, while comprehensive CCTV or solar systems may take 1-3 days. We'll provide a timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance and support for all our installations. Regular maintenance ensures your security systems operate at peak performance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, EFT, and card payments. Payment terms can be discussed during your consultation.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we understand that security issues can't wait. Contact us for emergency repairs and urgent installations. We'll do our best to respond quickly to your needs.

Can I view my CCTV cameras remotely?

Yes, our modern CCTV systems include remote viewing capabilities. You can access your cameras from anywhere using a smartphone app or web browser.
